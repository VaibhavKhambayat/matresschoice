30 Nights Trial

MatressChoice offers a 30 nights trial for the MatressChoice mattress sold on the website. For all eligible purchases, customers are advised to contact customer service within 30 calendar days from the time of purchase. Cost of shipping will be borne by MatressChoice so long as the product has not been moved out of the premises of the original delivery location. In all other cases, the customer will bear the differential shipping charges.

We reserve the right to change or withdraw the 30 nights trial program at any time.

Process For Returns

Please write to enquiry@MatressChoice.com with your order details within 30 calendar days from date of purchase to register your return request.

Exceptions to our policies (Warranty & 30 Nights Trial)

  • Any damage or defect not covered under the manufacturer's warranty
  • The original invoice with the company's seal and signature has to be presented for all warranty and return requests
  • Risk free trial is not available on customized sizes
