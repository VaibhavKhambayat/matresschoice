Our Story

After 9 years in the mattress industry working with the worlds best luxury beds and mattress brands, we realised that there is a huge disparity between the high end brands and the local mattresses that most Indians sleep on.

Mattresses are one of those items on the list that received the least bit of attention while making homes. This has slowly changed over the years with more and more urban Indians realising the importance of good sleep and it's direct relation with good health.

We wanted to come up with a smart simple and effective sleep solution for the urban Indian. So we used all the 9 years of experience and spent many months working with designers to ensure that we had combinations of materials never seen before in the Indian market.

Considering that a lot of the superior quality raw materials that goes into mattresses are available right here in our country, we wanted MattressChoice products to be completely 'Made in India'

" Our vision was to give the customer the comfort of a top end mattress without making a deep hole in their pockets. We cut out the middleman and took our concept online so that we could offer our mattresses at a reasonable, affordable price."
