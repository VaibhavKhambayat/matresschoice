Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which mattress is best for me?

We have three mattresses to suit different needs, based on the features listed below; you can select the best fit.

First Sense

A relatively firm mattress that has been specially designed keeping in mind the science of sleep, posture and support for the human body.

It is recommended for those prone to back and spine issues. People with sedentary job with hours spent staring at the computer & people who sleep on their back can all benefit more from a firm mattress as it offers utmost support to the spine and properly aligns it through the night as you sleep.

A firm mattress is recommended by chiropractors to those who have a history of back and neck problems.

Dual Sense

A mattress that offers the height of luxury, with a top layer of memory foam, this is perfect if you think of your bed as a cosy, inviting space, one that you can crawl into and float away on a cloud of dreams. A relatively soft mattress that is recommended for not just the joy of sleeping, but also for those who sleep on their side, people who suffer from joint pain (the lower resistance a relatively soft mattress offers helps soothe aching joints).

Cloud Sense

Cloud Sense is a memory foam mattress that takes on the contours of your body and provides your spine with support and a snug comfort. Experts consider good quality memory foam as an ideal component for ergonomic mattresses. For those looking for back relief, spinal cord specialists recommend Memory Foam as it cradles the body's curves while also being firm enough to support the backs muscular and bony structures.

How does the 30-NIGHTS Trial Offer work?

Once you have purchased our mattress, you have 30 nights to decide whether this is the right mattress for you.

If it’s not the right mattress (we know that’s impossible, our mattresses are designed to make you fall in love with them), you can request for a refund and we’ll take care of the rest.

The mattress must be in undamaged condition is all we request from you. No spillages, or stains, rips or ragged bits.

How are your prices so competitive?
